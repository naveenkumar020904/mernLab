About Us

Prasad V Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology (PVPSIT) is a premier self-financed institution established in 1998. It is renowned for academic excellence and offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. The campus spans 19.98 acres and is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories and infrastructure. The institution is accredited by NAAC with an A+ grade and is ISO 9001-2015 certified.

The college provides various research, innovation, and entrepreneurial opportunities for students. Our robust placement cell helps students secure career opportunities, with the highest package reaching 44 lakhs. The college encourages students' holistic development, emphasizing academic growth, extracurricular activities, and social responsibility.
